Ingram Micro SSC

Worked as a Software Developer in Ingram Micro SSC Pvt. Ltd.

IMOnline is the B2B e-Store which is used by retail partners of Ingram Micro for ordering their stock of electronics

IMOnline Mobile Application has been released for 11 countries and the web version is available for approximately 26 countries. With continuous rollout of the application, the mobile application will soon be available for all the rest of the countries.

IMOnline Mobile Application has the following features :-

  • Search for products and get their latest price and availability based on your credit history.
  • Order products online. Buy products in large quantities on credit, no form of payment required.
  • Track your Orders and Return status.

Modules developed by me :-

  • Product Search and Product Details
  • Country Selection
  • Wishlist
  • Order Details
  • Video Demo


IMJarvis is a web/mobile chatbot used by the sales executives of Ingram Micro to easily get order details, product details and return status for any customer of theirs. The login allows them to have access only of order and return details of theit customers. The login is the AD Credentials that they otherwise use to sign in to their workstations.

Participated in the IBM-Call for Code - Global Hackathon with Forest Fire Monitoring and Alerting System. It is a system consisting of Watson IOT, IBM Cloudant Database and Image Recognition. The idea was about alerting authorities about forest fire through a combination of temperature measurement, capturing image of the scene and sending it to the Cloudant DB and then running an image recognition to understand if there is a fire or the temperature change is natural.
