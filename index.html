How "Come Alive Cards" work?

Come Alive Cards are used to turn "wow" into "wooooow"
Here is what you have to do

Download

Download whichever card design you like from our ever growing collection of greeting cards. The image will be download in the highest resolution available to us, with no watermarks.

Print

Print it on a paper and stick it inside your card or use the image for printing in the card you are making. Just make sure you keep the print in color and quality nice & clear.

Share

After gifting your card, share the link to our Android application with the person you are giving the card to. Alternatively if you are with the person you can also show the app on your phone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Come Alive Cards mobile app work on all Android versions?

The application provides support from Android version 4.1 (Jellybean) upto the latest Android version which is about 99% of the total Android devices.

Does Come Alive Cards run on iOS?

No, currently we only support Android Devices and in the future may extend support to iOS devices, although for a personalized card we can provide an iOS application on the client's AppStore account (contact for further details).

Does it require an active internet connection?

Once you have the application downloaded to your phone you do not need an internet connection, you can view the animation without an internet connection.

For a personalized card, how much time does it take to deliver?

After the image and the animation has been finalized the card will be delivered within a maximum of 7 days in case of Standard plan and 10 days in case of Family plan. Although it should be noted that distribution via Playstore requires much more time and these timelines are applicable for APK sharing via Google Drive or any other medium suitable to the client.
