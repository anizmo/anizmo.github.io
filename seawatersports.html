Sea Water Sports

Android Application Development and Web Development.

About Sea Water Sports :

Sea Water Sports is a travel app with suggestions and other useful things in it.
Get useful information like restaurants, bars, pubs, markets, places to visit, the best time to visit and much more.
Informative material which is also unique is made and written by smart local guides.
Discover the wide range of travel experiences on the app. The experiences include romantic, adventurous, trekking, peaceful, and much more.

My contribution towards Sea Water Sports:-

  • End to End development of their Customer facing Android Application.
  • End to End development of their internal CRM Android Application.
  • Managing release on playstore and providing ASO services.
  • Acting as a tech-consultant to Sea Water Sports when dealing with other service providers.

The Android Application is developed with a minimum API requirement of API 16 i.e. Android Jellybean to ensure maximum number of available devices can run it. At the same time, the app uses the latest Android 10, API 29 compile tools and support library to give the users with latest Android Phones the best possible experience.
